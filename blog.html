How to Effectively Protect a Website From Hackers

Cybercrime and hacking have become all too common in today’s online world, and even the statistics are frightening. There’s an ever-growing need to protect ourselves, our private information, and our data in such a world. Website owners know this better than anyone. Today, they must be even more vigilant than ever, as they’re responsible for other people’s data. That’s why running a successful website today requires much more than making one. You’ll need sufficient security measures to stay ahead of malicious users and protect your website from growing data breaches and hacks.

    10 ways you can instantly protect your website from hackers

  • 1. Use important security plugins
  • 2. Update everything regularly
  • 3. Add HTTPS to your site
  • 4. Secure passwords and use information responsibly
  • 5. Secure file permissions
  • 6. Perform automatic backups
  • 7. Scan your website regularly
  • 8. Use a firewall
  • 9. Avoid plugins with security vulnerabilities
  • 10. Add two-factor authentication
